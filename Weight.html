Weight Loss Plan Overviews

Lose Weight

Formula and Calculation: How to measure your BMI in Kg or Lbs?

Kilograms Formula

Formula: weight (kg) / [height (m)]2
Let saty your Weight = 80 kg, Height = 170 cm (1.7 m) Calculation: 80 ÷ (1.7)2 = 27.68

Repeat the above formula for Pounds:

Formula: weight (lb) / [height (in)]2 x 703
Example: Weight = 200 lbs, Height = 5’9″ (65″) Calculation: [200 ÷ (71)2] x 703 = 27.90

How to Measure Your Waist?

Get a waist measurement, depending of the size of your tummy
Place the tape measure at zero until it reaches and/or indicates the number of your waist in inches/centimeters.
Double-check for accuracy. A healthy man waist should be less than ( > 37 in (94 cm) ) or
less than ( > 31.5 in (80 cm) ) for a healthy woman waist as average indicator of low risk of serious medical problems to be at low risk of obesity => type 2 diabetes, high blood pressure/hypertension, high cholesterol; those factors can limit blood flow, increase risk of stroke or heart attack.
